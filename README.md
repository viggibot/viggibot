## 🎯 Current Focus & Startups

### 🚁 Navi3D & Drone Engineering
* **Navi3D Drone Studio:** Developing a 3D web application utilizing **Three.js and Threlte** to visually assemble drones and flash custom **Teensy microcontrollers** directly from the browser. 
* **Custom Micro-Drones:** Engineering topology-optimized micro-drone frames using precision **SLA 3D printing technology** (specifically avoiding standard FDM for better tolerances). Currently dialing in custom builds featuring Q450 frames in a **"Props In" Quad X configuration**, driven by 8520 coreless motors.
* **Electronics Design:** Routing custom PCBs for coreless motors, integrating power management via MOSFETs, PWM, and boost converter circuits (MT3608/TPS61023) for optimal power delivery.
* **Gemini Sync (NaviSync):** Building a 5km custom proprietary digital HD video transmission link leveraging RTL8812AU chipsets and OpenHD/OpenIPC firmware for low-latency drone telemetry.

### 🕉️ Artyla & In-House Manufacturing
* **Unified Architecture:** Architected and deployed the complete technical infrastructure and unified **SvelteKit & Rust** admin dashboard that powers both Navi3D and Artyla.
* **SLA Bulk Production:** Personally designing cultural 3D models and murthis (including Ganesha, Durga Maa, and Natraj) for retail and manufacturing. 
* **Cost Engineering:** Engineered the proprietary costing models for bulk SLA manufacturing, optimizing for scale where material and fulfillment costs multiply per unit, but machine-level overhead (electricity, cleaning agents) remains flat for the batch.
